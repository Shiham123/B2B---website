EXPERIENCE THE GOLD STANDARD IN RECRUITING FOR B2B SERVICES

RELATIONSHIPS COME FIRST

We take time to learn what makes you and your company unique to a prospective candidate. We want to understand the nuances of your organization’s culture, values, history, growth potential, senior leadership, and more. Careful fit builds lasting relationships.

ACCESS TO HARD-TO-FIND CANDIDATES

The most qualified candidates are usually happily employed. That's why we seek out "passive" candidates: leaders who are employed and not necessarily in the market for a new job. We can ensure our candidates will be the best in their fields.

INTERVIEWING BY INDUSTRY INSIDERS

Wouldn’t you rather have interviewing done by an industry leader? If you're hiring a VP, we have a VP talk to the candidates. If you seek a GM, we have someone with GM experience interview them. It's a unique advantage of our decades of industry experience.

QUALITY OVER QUANTITY

Before you even see a resumé, our candidates will have been through a rigorous interview process. We screen based on your qualifications, significantly reducing candidates that do not fit. You'll know you're meeting with the best.

STEP-BY-STEP SCHEDULING

Unlike agencies that hand over the reins at the interview stage and cross their fingers that their candidates do well, we work with you and the candidates throughout the entire interview process to coordinate the next steps and be sure things are running smoothly.

CAREER MENTORING

When you find the right candidate, they become a part of your family. We like to serve as a resource throughout their entire career with your company. This support is essential in the first six months when candidates are getting settled into their new roles.
